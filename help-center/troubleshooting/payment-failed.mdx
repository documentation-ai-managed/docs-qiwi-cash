---
title: Payment Failed to Process
description: How to fix a payment failure in Qiwi Cash.
---

## Issue
- Payment shows as failed or rejected
- Transaction not completed

## Causes
- Insufficient balance
- Incorrect recipient details

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check balance">
    ````bash
    curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" https://api.qiwi.cash/v1/balance
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify recipient">
    Make sure the recipient wallet ID is valid and active.
  </Step>
</Steps>
