## Qiwi Wallet Creation

Follow these steps to set up your first wallet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet Page">
    Login at `https://cabinet.qiwi.cash`, select **Wallets**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Wallet">
    Click **Create Wallet**, enter a name, and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Wallet ID">
    Your new wallet ID will appear in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
