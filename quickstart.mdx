## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An active email address for verification
- Optional: A business domain for custom branding

</Callout>

Qiwi Cash works entirely in the browser, so no downloads are required.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and get verified quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [https://qiwi-cash.com](https://qiwi-cash.com) and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your email, create a strong password, and agree to the terms.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">

    Check your inbox for the verification email from Qiwi Cash.

    Click the link to activate your account. It expires in 24 hours.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">

    Return to the dashboard and add your name, company details, and profile picture.

    Enable two-factor authentication for security.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Dashboard

Once logged in, you land on the main dashboard at `https://dashboard.qiwi-cash.com`.

Key sections include:

- **Spaces**: Manage your documentation projects
- **Documents**: Upload and organize files
- **Templates**: Pre-built starters
- **Settings**: Account and billing

Use the search bar to find content quickly.

## Set Up Your First Project Space

Create a dedicated space for your project documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="plus">

    Click **New Space** in the sidebar.

    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and select a template (e.g., "API Reference").

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">

    Set permissions: public, private, or team-only.

    Customize the URL slug, e.g., `qiwi-cash.com/my-project`.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Initial Documents

Add your first files using these methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="upload">

    Open your space and drag files directly into the editor.

    Supported formats: Markdown (`.mdx`), PDF, images (PNG, JPG), and ZIP archives.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="URL Import" icon="globe">

    Paste a public URL to any Markdown or GitHub file.

    Qiwi Cash fetches and converts it automatically.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bulk Upload" icon="package">

    Use the bulk importer for folders.

    Maintains folder structure and links.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Basic Workspace Customization

Tailor your space to match your brand.

<Expandable title="Theme Colors" default-open="true">

  Go to **Space Settings > Theme**.

  Set primary color to `#3B82F6` (Qiwi Cash blue).

  Upload a logo and favicon.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Navigation Menu">

  Edit the sidebar in **Space Settings > Navigation**.

  Add custom links like `/api` or `/changelog`.

  Drag to reorder.

</Expandable>

For advanced custom CSS, see the [full customization guide](/configuration).
